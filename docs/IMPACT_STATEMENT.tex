\vspace{0.3cm}

Sales is a \$900 billion industry employing over 13 million people in the US alone. Yet the training methods haven't evolved in decades:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \textbf{Books and videos} don't build skills — sales requires practice
\item \textbf{Manager roleplay} is limited, inconsistent, and biased
\item \textbf{Real prospects} become unwitting practice targets — costing deals
\item \textbf{3-6 month ramp time} for new reps, with high failure rates
\end{itemize}

The result: \textbf{\$97,690 average cost} to replace a failed sales rep, and millions in lost revenue from poorly trained teams.

% -------------------- Why Voice AI Matters --------------------
\section{Why Voice AI is Essential}

Text-based training can't replicate real sales conversations:

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{p{5cm}p{7cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Text/Chatbot Training} & \textbf{Voice AI Training (Sparrow)}\\
\midrule
Typing isn't talking & Natural speech patterns\\
No vocal tone practice & Real prosody and pacing\\
Can think before responding & Real-time pressure simulation\\
Misses 38\% of communication & Full verbal communication practice\\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

Research shows that 38\% of communication is vocal tone. Sales success depends on \textit{how} you say things, not just what you say. Only voice AI can train this effectively.
