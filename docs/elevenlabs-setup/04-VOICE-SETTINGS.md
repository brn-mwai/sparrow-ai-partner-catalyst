# ElevenLabs Agent - Voice Settings

Configure the voices for your agent.

---

## Step 1: Access Voice Settings

1. Look at the right sidebar
2. Find the **"Voices"** section
3. Click on it to expand

---

## Step 2: Set Primary Voice

1. Click on the current voice or **"Add additional voice"**
2. Search for: **Eric**
3. Select it
4. Click **"Set as Primary"**

---

## Step 3: Add Additional Voices

Add these voices for variety:

### Voice 1: Eric (Primary - Male)
- Good for: Skeptical, professional male personas
- Set as: **Primary**

### Voice 2: Rachel (Female)
- Search: "Rachel"
- Good for: Friendly, warm female personas

### Voice 3: Adam (Male)
- Search: "Adam"
- Good for: Authoritative, busy male personas

### Voice 4: Bella (Female)
- Search: "Bella"
- Good for: Professional, direct female personas

---

## Step 4: Select LLM

1. In the right sidebar, find **"LLM"** section
2. Click on it
3. Select: **Gemini 2.5 Flash**

---

## Step 5: Set Language

1. In the right sidebar, find **"Language"** section
2. Make sure **English** is set as Default
